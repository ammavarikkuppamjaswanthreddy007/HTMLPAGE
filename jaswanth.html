Jaswanth

Sep MJK Reddy was given the gallantry award, “Shaurya Chakra” posthumously on 26 Jan 2022, for his commendable bravery, devotion to duty, camaraderie and supreme sacrifice. Sep Maruprolu Jaswanth Kumar Reddy is survived by his father Shri M Srinivasa Reddy, mother Smt Venkateswaramma and two younger brothers.
